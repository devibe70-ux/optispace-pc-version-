# Privacy Policy for OptiSpace AI

**Effective Date:** June 28, 2026

## 1. 100% On-Device Processing
OptiSpace AI is built with a strict "Privacy-First" architecture. **All file scanning, AI visual deduplication, and video compression are performed 100% locally on your device.** 

We do not upload, transmit, or store your personal files, images, or videos on any external servers. Your data never leaves your computer.

## 2. Data Collection
OptiSpace AI does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require an account to use the core features of the app. 

### Third-Party Services
To support the development of this free application, we may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense/AdMob. These third-party services may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized ads. 
- You can manage your ad personalization preferences or opt-out of data sharing directly within the app via the "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link in the footer, in full compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and CPRA.

## 3. Permissions
The application requires local file system access exclusively to scan for duplicate files and compress videos at your explicit request. The app will never modify or delete a file without your direct confirmation.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact the developer team at **devibe70@gmail.com**.
